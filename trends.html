Summer 2024 Fashion Analysis.

What’s Hot this Summer?

Trends Discovered

Euro Summer

The "Euro Summer" trend is all about embodying the effortless chic and relaxed elegance found on the sun-soaked streets and beaches of Europe's most fashionable destinations. Think lightweight, flowy dresses in neutral tones, classic Breton stripes, and swimwear paired with lacey cover ups for functionality. This trend embraces natural fabrics like linen and cotton, ideal for keeping cool while looking effortlessly put together. Accessories are minimal yet impactful, seeing sites of delicate jewelry to add the perfect finishing touches. Whether strolling through a quaint Mediterranean town or lounging by the sea, the European Summer aesthetic is a timeless blend of comfort, sophistication, and understated glamour.

Everything White!!!

White Everything is the epitome of elegance and simplicity, embracing the versatility and timelessness of an all-white wardrobe. This trend highlights the effortless sophistication that comes with wearing white from head to toe, perfect for both summer days and chic evenings. Whether it's a flowing dress, tailored shorts, or a crisp blouse, white adds a touch of freshness and modernity to any outfit. This trend is not only about making a fashion statement but also about embracing a minimalist aesthetic that exudes old money and new experiences.

Corporate, Casual, and Chic

The Corporate, Casual, and Chic trend seamlessly blends professional attire with casual comfort, creating looks that are both polished and relaxed. Imagine neutral oversized blazers paired with relaxed trousers or a crisp button-down worn with sleek jeans. This trend is perfect for those who want to stay stylish in the office while being ready for a spontaneous outing after work. It’s all about balance, where sharp tailoring meets soft, cozy fabrics.

The PERFECT Black Dress

Timeless, elegant, and versatile, the little black dress (LBD) is a wardrobe essential that never goes out of style. Whether you're heading to a sophisticated evening event or just want to elevate your day-to-day look, the LBD adapts effortlessly to any occasion. This timeless look focuses on sleek silhouettes, flattering cuts, and subtle yet impactful details like lace accents, daring necklines, and intricate textures. The perfect black dress is not just a piece of clothing; it’s a statement of confidence and style that speaks volumes with its simplicity and grace.

Project Breakdown

Apify: Insta Post Scraper

I scraped the latest 50 posts from the top 40 fashion accounts on Instagram using Apify, allowing me to gather a rich dataset of images that reflect current summer fashion trends. An automated scraping process was essential in collecting real-time data, which I later used for detailed image analysis and trend forecasting.

Image Segmentation

I utilized image segmentation to isolate specific elements within the fashion images, such as clothing items or accessories. By separating these elements from the background, I enhanced the visual clarity of the data, making it easier to categorize and analyze different fashion trends. This step was crucial in ensuring the accuracy of the trend identification process.

Latent Space Creation

I created a latent space representation of the segmented images using dimensionality reduction techniques like PCA. This transformation reduced the complexity of the data while retaining key features, allowing me to efficiently group similar images together, making it a crucial step in identifying underlying patterns in current fashion trends.

K-Means Clustering

I applied K-Means Clustering to the latent space representations to group together outfits with similar characteristics. By clustering these images, I was able to identify distinct fashion trends and visually represent how different styles are related. This method was integral to analyzing and understanding the connections between various fashion trends in the dataset.
